I know, no one likes nonstop whining.

Jul 17, 2014

The funny thing is, when others are complaining, I will try my best to comfort them; when I am complaining, people just tell me “stop complaining, move on plzzzzzz.”

And then when my friends talk about me they’re like, “She never complains about stuff or even seems stressed! Either she has a big heart or she really is not stressed at all.”

Hey, my life is no easier than yours, all right? If you can complain, I can too. And I am not, like, stronger than you. Why the hell is that everyone else could complain but not me?

I know I should try harder instead of complaining. I just don’t think that I have no rights to do what some others always do. Okay you can ignore me the whole point of this post is complaining byebye
