Things I really really want to do before I die - II

Here’s my last “Things I really really want to do before I die”, written two years ago. Happy to say that I’ve accomplished some “things” in the first list, and added more bullets to it.

Here we go again, and hopefully I can check some of them off in two years time…

  • Take a gap year or half a year to just to enjoy life. Hopefully roaming around the world
  • Sell my own web/mobile app
  • Earn money from a hobby (writing, tourguiding, photography, piano playing… anything)
  • Visit Venice - Not started
  • Publish a novel - IP
  • Become an Airbnb host or hostel owner… okay this one is far far away…

“Really wanna do” list which contains stuff that almost makes the above list, but depends toooo much on opportunity:
